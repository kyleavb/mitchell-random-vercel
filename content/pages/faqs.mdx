    items:
      - question: "What programs are available?"
        answer: "Mitchell College offers two fully online B.A. degree completion programs: <strong>Business</strong> and <strong>Health and Human Services</strong>. Both are designed for adult learners with 60 or more transfer credits."
      - question: "Are the programs fully online?"
        answer: "Yes. All courses are delivered asynchronously online. There are no required live lectures or campus visits. You can access course materials and complete assignments on your own schedule."
      - question: "When can I start?"
        answer: "Mitchell offers flexible entry points throughout the year — you can begin in the spring, summer, or fall term. Rolling admissions means there's no rigid application deadline."
      - question: "Can I attend part-time?"
        answer: "Absolutely. You can enroll full-time (two courses per term) or part-time (one course per term), and switch between the two as your schedule requires."
      - question: "Are there additional course options?"
        answer: "Yes. Mitchell offers 4-week Intersession courses in winter and summer, giving you the opportunity to earn additional credits on an accelerated timeline."
